How Our Moving Estimates Work

Honest Pricing. Clear Expectations. No Surprises.

← Back to Home

At GwinnettMovers.com, we believe a great move starts with a great estimate.

That’s why we take the time to understand your home, your belongings, and your situation—so you get a price that’s accurate, fair, and stress‑free on moving day.

What a Professional Moving Estimate Really Means

A moving estimate is a custom plan, not a guess.

When we provide an estimate, we’re calculating:

  • The time your move will take
  • The number of movers required
  • The equipment needed to protect your belongings
  • The safest and most efficient way to complete your move

This lets us price your move based on real conditions, not generic assumptions.

How We Create Accurate Estimates

We don’t believe in one‑size‑fits‑all pricing. Every estimate is based on details that actually matter.

We look at:

  • The size of your home or office
  • Furniture and large items
  • Estimated number of boxes
  • Stairs, elevators, and walking distance
  • Access, parking, and building rules
  • Distance between locations
  • Any special packing or handling needs

The more we know upfront, the smoother—and more accurate—your move will be.

In‑Home, Virtual, or Phone Estimates

Choose What Works for You

To make things easy, we offer multiple estimate options:

  • In‑home estimates for maximum accuracy
  • Virtual walkthroughs using your phone or video call
  • Detailed phone estimates for smaller or simpler moves

Our goal is always the same: clear expectations and honest pricing.

Understanding Your Estimate Type

Non‑Binding Estimates (Most Local Moves)

This is the most common option for local moves in Gwinnett County.

  • Based on expected time and labor
  • Final price depends on actual hours worked
  • Can go up or down depending on conditions

This approach keeps pricing flexible and fair—especially when timing or access may change.

Binding / Guaranteed Estimates (When Applicable)

For certain moves, we can offer a flat, guaranteed price.

  • Requires a detailed inventory
  • Price remains the same as long as nothing changes
  • Added items or new conditions may adjust the quote

We’ll always explain your options clearly before you decide.

Why Our Estimates Are Trustworthy

Some movers win jobs with unrealistically low quotes—then make it up on moving day.

We do things differently.

  • No rushed estimates
  • No hidden fees
  • No surprise add‑ons
  • No pressure tactics

If we quote you a price, it’s because we’ve done the homework to stand behind it.

How You Can Help Get the Best Estimate

Want the most accurate quote possible? Here’s how to help:

  • Be honest about what you’re moving
  • Include garages, storage units, and sheds
  • Mention stairs, elevators, or tight access
  • Don’t underestimate boxes
  • Tell us about specialty items upfront

The better the information, the smoother the move.
